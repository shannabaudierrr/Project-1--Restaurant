Our Story

Since 1934, our family has been serving local fans and travelers our own versions of diner classics. Our extensive menu, accented with Tex-Mex influences and Seasonal Specials, reflects our commitment to offer high quality fresh ingredients. We provide our guests with generous portions at a reasonable price in a comfortable modern diner setting.

Our second franchise, was opened in 1957 and is owned by our fellow family member, Hunter Smith. We now have 16 franchised Roxy's Grills in 6 different states. We strive to open more franchises in the near future in order to give our loyal customers a comfortable restaurant atmosphere with great prices.

Giving the customers satisfaction is our goal. As a family-run business, we want to make anyone who walks through our doors feel welcomed and comfortable. Our recipes even remind most customers of home cooked meals. You can always get a taste of home when you eat here. We never let a customer leave upset. In 2013, Roxy’s Grill was named “Alabama’s Best Family-Owned Business.”

We serve breakfast any time of the day in order to make our restaurant truly feel like home. No matter how far from home, you can always come to Roxy’s Grill for a feel for home. All of our customers are an extension of our family and we truly appreciate the love and support we have received over the years.
